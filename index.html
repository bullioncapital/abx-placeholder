Redefining the way the world trades physical

Allocated Bullion Exchange (ABX) is the world's first electronic exchange for allocated physical precious metals. It brings price discovery, intitutional trading and clearing facilities into one online trading enviroment via its ground-breaking technological solution, the MetalDesk exchange trading platform. ABX gives market participants everywhere direct access to the wholesale bullion market, connecting a worldwide network of liquidity providers and takers.
